# Ankayma — User Guide

How to use each part of the Ankayma app. Ankayma puts your machines and services on a
private mesh: traffic goes **directly between your devices** (encrypted, peer-to-peer)
and never through the vendor — the control plane only brokers identity and policy, and
you can **Prove It**.

**New here? Start with [Getting started](getting-started.md)** — from sign-in to your
first proof (SSH from your phone into your laptop) in about five minutes.

## The journey

### 1. Start here
- [Getting started](getting-started.md) — install, sign in (**NoPass**), and your first win.

### 2. Grow your mesh
- [Devices](devices.md) — enroll, view, and remove the machines on your mesh.
- [SSH](ssh.md) — **NoKey**: SSH with no bastion and no static key.
- [Subdomains](subdomains.md) — **NoVPN**: give a service a private name.
- [Services](services.md) — see which private services you're allowed to reach.
- [Deploy Rules](deploy-rules.md) — **NoSecret**: secretless CI/CD from GitHub/GitLab.
- [Prove it](prove-it.md) — **Prove It**: verify the vendor wasn't on your data path.

### 3. Secure it
- [Security & two-factor](security.md) — a TOTP step-up factor for sensitive actions.

### 4. Grow up
- [Upgrade & grow](upgrade.md) — F0 → F0-Plus, or start an F1 team.
- [Tiers](tiers.md) — what each tier includes, side by side.

### 5. Bring a team
- [Members](members.md) — invite teammates and manage roles (team tier).
- [Access](access.md) — author who-can-reach-what policy (team tier).

## Tiers in one line

- **F0 (free):** your own devices, just you, private names, a secretless deploy taste.
- **F0-Plus (personal):** more nodes and names, raw TCP tunneling, step-up 2FA.
- **F1 (team):** invite members, shared access policy, and an admin console.

Full breakdown: [Tiers](tiers.md).

## A note on honesty

Where a feature isn't fully live yet, the guide says so plainly — Ankayma marks
unfinished edges rather than hiding them.
