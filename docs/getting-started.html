Getting started

Ankayma puts your machines on a private mesh where they reach each other directly — no VPN, no public ports — and you can prove the vendor was never in the middle. No credit card: signing in creates your own free F0 space.

Download Ankayma — it's free macOS · Windows · Linux · iOS

Show your product to a client — privately (5 minutes)

Give a client or partner a private, professional look at your work — they open it themselves, no VPN, no public link — and you can prove the vendor never saw it. This is exactly how you demo to prospects and partners.

Get on your mesh — a private network only you control. ~2 min

  1. Install the Ankayma app and open it — no IT, no infrastructure to set up.
  2. Click GitHub to sign in. This first sign-in creates your free F0 space. (No GitHub? We set you up by email — see the full guide.)
  3. Click Connect. macOS asks for your password once. You'll see "You're in the mesh".

Put your demo on a private name — branded, no public port. ~1 min

  1. The easy way — Publish a sample demo. On Subdomains, click Publish a sample demo: Ankayma serves a ready-made page on a branded private name — no server to run, no terminal.
  2. Or show your own app. Already running a web app or dashboard on your machine? Add a subdomain and point it at that local port — your real product, on a private name. (This is NoVPN.)
  3. Either way, only invited devices resolve it — no public DNS, no open port.

Invite your client — one link, no password. ~1 min

  1. Click Invite someone to view — you get a QR or a link.
  2. Send it to your client. They install Ankayma and scan it — their device joins with no password. (This is NoPass.)
  3. They open your demo themselves — privately, with no public link for anyone else to find.

Prove It — the trust close for security-minded clients. ~30 s

  1. On Services, tap ◈ path next to your demo.
  2. Read the path chain: direct peer-to-peer, the real endpoint, and Vendor in path: No ✓.
  3. You can show a client the connection is private and the vendor never saw it — that's a real differentiator for enterprise buyers.

◈ Proof: no public link, no open port — and the path chain shows the vendor was never in the middle.

That's the whole business: show your work to clients, privately, on your terms.
