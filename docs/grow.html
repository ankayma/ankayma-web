Grow & plans

Upgrade when you've proven the value — not because a banner told you to.

You've already validated it

By the time growth makes sense, you've seen it work for yourself:

  • On Free (F0) you proved the flow end-to-end — a private demo, a client who opened it, and proof the vendor was never on the path.
  • On a team (F1) you proved the business value: real clients and partners saw your work, privately, and it moved deals.

Then the need expands on its own.

The path

One mesh grows with you — pick where you are:

F0 — Free start here · $0

  • Your own devices, private names, a secretless deploy taste.
  • Show a private demo to a client, end to end — at $0.

F0-Plus — more room for one when you hit the free caps

  • More nodes and private names, raw-TCP tunneling, a second factor.
  • Unlocked in the app — Settings → Upgrade.

F1 Team — your team on one mesh when it's not just you

  • Roles and shared access; a sales team demoing to many prospects.
  • Invite & offboard in one click. Self-serve in the app.

F1 Sales — a field force many low-touch viewers

  • Lite User seats for an agent network: agents, franchise staff, clients — many people who just view.
  • Shaped around your rollout — talk to us.

Enterprise — a separate product line regulated / isolation

  • Dedicated infrastructure, your own tenant CA, compliance-grade isolation.
  • A guided onboarding with our team — see Enterprise.

How to upgrade

F0-Plus and F1 Team are self-serve inside the app (Settings → Upgrade) — your account is bound to the checkout automatically. F1 Sales and Enterprise are a quick conversation, because they're shaped around your rollout, not a button.
