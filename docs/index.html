User Guide

Show your product to a client — privately — and grow when the value is proven. These pages are the short version; the full reference lives on the docs site.

The story: Get started → put your demo on a private name → invite a client → prove it → grow.

The core journey

  • Getting started start here
    Show your product to a client — privately — in five minutes, ending in proof the vendor never saw it.
  • Services & private domains
    NoVPN — put your app or demo on a branded private name only invited devices can reach.
  • NoPass — invite & team
    Let a client into your demo with no password; bring your team in when the value is proven.
  • Grow & plans
    Upgrade after you've proven it works: F0-Plus, F1 Team, F1 Sales, Enterprise.

Advanced

Go deeper — for the dev, security, or IT people who vet the product.

  • NoSecret CI/CD
    Deploy from CI with no static secret — every run ends in a signed receipt.

    Passwordless sign-in, a TOTP second factor, and step-up on sensitive actions.

    Who can reach what — default-deny, on real identity, tamper-evident.
