User Guide

From your first win to a team on the mesh — how to use every part of Ankayma. Traffic goes directly between your devices, and you can prove it.

The journey: Start here → Grow your mesh → Secure it → Grow up → Bring a team

1 · Start here

  • Getting started live
    Install, sign in (NoPass), and your first win — NoKey SSH from your phone into your laptop, ending in Prove It.

2 · Grow your mesh

  • Devices soon
    Enroll, view, and remove the machines on your mesh.
  • SSH — NoKey soon
    SSH into your machines with no bastion and no static key.
  • Subdomains — NoVPN soon
    Give a service a private name only your mesh resolves.
  • Services soon
    See which private services you're allowed to reach.
  • Deploy Rules — NoSecret soon
    Secretless CI/CD deploy from GitHub or GitLab.
  • Prove It soon
    Verify, live, that the vendor was never on your data path.

3 · Secure it

  • Security & two-factor soon
    Add a TOTP step-up factor (optional YubiKey) for sensitive actions.

4 · Grow up

  • Upgrade & grow soon
    F0 → F0-Plus (more room), or start an F1 Team.
  • Tiers soon
    What each tier includes, side by side.

5 · Bring a team

  • Members soon
    Invite teammates and manage roles (team tier).
  • Access soon
    Author who-can-reach-what policy (team tier).

Tiers in one line

  • F0 (free) — your own devices, private names, a secretless deploy taste.
  • F0-Plus (personal) — more nodes and names, raw TCP tunneling, step-up 2FA.
  • F1 (team) — invite members, shared access policy, and an admin console.

Where a feature isn't fully live yet, the guide says so plainly — Ankayma marks unfinished edges rather than hiding them. Pages tagged soon are being written.
