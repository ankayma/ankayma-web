NoPass — invite & team

Let a client into your private demo with one link and no password — then grow into a team once the value is proven.

Show it to a client

This is the point of the whole thing: a client or partner opens your private demo themselves, on their own device, with no password to manage and no public link for anyone else to find.

  1. Click Invite someone to view — you get a QR or a link.
  2. Send it. They install Ankayma and scan it; their device joins with no password (their device is the credential).
  3. They open your demo — private, professional, and yours to revoke the moment the deal's done.

Pair this with a service on a private name (Services / NoVPN): that's the necessary half, this is the sufficient half.

Bring your team in

When you're not doing this alone, invite a teammate the same way. Everyone joins one mesh under a role you choose, and offboarding is one click — their next request loses access immediately.

When it grows — you'll feel it

You don't upgrade because a banner told you to; you upgrade because it's working:

  • More clients or projects → move from Free to a personal plan or an F1 Team. See Grow & plans.
  • A field force or agent network — many low-touch viewers (agents, franchise staff, clients) → F1 Sales with Lite User seats. Talk to us.
