# Upgrade & grow

Free **F0** is a real product, not a trial — your own machines, **NoVPN** private names,
**NoKey** SSH, a **NoSecret** deploy taste, and **Prove It**. When you outgrow it, there
are two ways to grow: stay solo on **F0-Plus**, or bring people in on an **F1 Team** plan.
Both start from **Upgrade** in the app.

Upgrading keeps your existing mesh exactly as it is — **same keys, no re-enrollment**.

## F0-Plus — more room for you (personal)

**$9 / month.** For one person who's hitting the free limits or wants a second factor:

- **50 mesh nodes** (up from 10).
- **20 private domains** (up from 5) — more **NoVPN** names for your services.
- **Raw TCP tunneling** — any protocol, not just HTTP/S.
- **Step-up 2FA** — a TOTP second factor, with an optional YubiKey. See [Security](security.md).
- Your existing mesh continues unchanged.

## F1 Team — bring people in (multi-user)

For a team. You pick a **seat pack** by team size:

| Pack | Price | Admins | Members |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3 | $19 / mo | 1 | up to 3 |
| 5 | $29 / mo | 1 | up to 5 |
| 10 | $49 / mo | 2 | up to 10 |
| 25 | $99 / mo | 3 | up to 25 |

Every F1 plan includes **everything in F0-Plus**, plus:

- **Team members** — invite people up to your seat count. See [Members](members.md).
- **Shared policies & access** — team-wide who-can-reach-what rules and roles. See
  [Access](access.md).
- **Admin console** — manage members, devices, and audit.

<!-- IMG: upgrade-02-f1-seats.png — The Upgrade screen, F1 Team tab, with the seat-pack picker (3/5/10/25) -->

Need more admins than a pack includes? Add an **admin seat** for **+$9/month** each from
**Settings → Account**.

## How to upgrade

1. Open **Upgrade** in the app.
2. Pick **F0-Plus** or the **F1 Team** seat pack you want.
3. Click **Subscribe**. Checkout opens in your browser, handled securely by **Lemon
   Squeezy** (our merchant of record — they handle billing and tax).
4. Finish payment and return to the app. It notices the change and switches you to the
   new tier automatically.

<!-- IMG: upgrade-03-checkout.png — The Lemon Squeezy checkout opened in the browser -->

Billing is monthly and you can **cancel anytime**. Your tenant is stamped into the
checkout from your session, so the payment activates *your* space and nobody else's.

> A downgrade or cancellation doesn't yank your mesh out from under you — the change is
> handled deliberately, not as an automatic cut-off.
