How it works

From install to proof in 5 minutes

Stand up the mesh, deploy to prod from CI with no static secret, and walk away with a signed receipt that proves it. Connection is a commodity — the proof is the product.

1
Sign up with GitHub One click. Your GitHub account gates access — no new password to manage.
2
Your devices enroll CLI on your server, app on your laptop and phone. The WireGuard keypair is generated locally — the private key never leaves your device, not even to us. Each device gets a stable overlay IP and a cert signed by your own tenant CA, and is your admin identity — no roles to wire up, no secret to store.
3
Set a deploy rule from anywhere From your laptop or your phone: agent ci-policy add --repo you/app --ref main --target prod. “This repo, this branch, deploys to this box.” Change it later the same way — no second ritual.
4
CI proves who it is — no static secret Your GitHub Action mints a short-lived OIDC token for that one run. Scoped, deploy-only, expires in minutes. Nothing static ever lives in your CI.
5
Keep the proof A signed run-receipt — which run, which repo, which service it touched — in a tamper-evident ledger. Press “Prove it” to see the real path: peer-to-peer, with the vendor never on it.

Press “Prove it.”

Most overlays can tell you it connected. None can hand you proof your traffic never touched the vendor. Ankayma shows the real path as an artifact you hold — not a promise you trust.

Honest about the edges: at F0 the receipt is tamper-evident (anchored in an append-only ledger). The ledger records connection-level facts — which run, which identity, which service it reached — not the commands run inside a session. Signing it with your own key plus an external witness is on the roadmap (Growth). Path-proof is absolute for direct peer-to-peer connections. See our honest limits →

Features

Zero trust, not zero control

Designed for developers who want security without giving up auditability.

🔒
WireGuard end-to-end

Modern cryptography. Data travels peer-to-peer, encrypted, never through our servers.

🔑
Your keys, your CA

Each tenant gets its own Certificate Authority. We sign your node certs; we can't impersonate your nodes.

📖
Open-source agent

The code running on your machines is fully auditable. Reproducible builds, Cosign-signed artifacts.

🌐
Edge channel (*.ankayma.net)

Expose any service in your mesh via a stable subdomain. No public IP, no port forwarding needed.

⚡
NAT traversal built-in

STUN + hole punching + relay fallback. Works behind NAT, firewalls, and mobile networks.

🛡️
Default-deny policy

Every connection requires explicit policy approval. No implicit trust between nodes.
