Getting started

Show your product to a client — privately, in five minutes

Give a client or partner a private, professional look at your work — they open it themselves, no VPN, no public link — and you can prove the vendor never saw it. This is exactly how you demo to prospects and partners.

1~2 minInstall and sign in — free F0, no IT — then Connect. You now have a private network only you control.
2~1 minPut the app or demo you're running on a branded private name — no public port, only invited devices can resolve it. This is NoVPN.
3~1 minInvite your client with one QR or link — their device joins with no password and they open your demo themselves. This is NoPass.
4~30 sProve It — show the connection is direct and the vendor was never on the path. The trust close for security-minded clients.

Solutions

Remove the credential. Keep the proof.

Each solution retires one kind of static credential — replaced by identity bound to the device, with an expiry and a ledger entry. Five doors, one mesh, two ways in. One honest choice among several, not the answer.

Path 1 You run the machines — bottom-up

An engineer, a repo, an agent of your own. Mostly live and self-serve — start where it bites.

NoSecret — CI/CD without static secrets

Every run gets a minutes-long identity and leaves a signed receipt — which run, which repo, which scope. Nothing static for a leak to find.
